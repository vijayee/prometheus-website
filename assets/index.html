Prometheus: The Social Currency Network

About

Prometheus was conceived out of an analysis of the fractional reserve banking system. The fractional reserve banking system as a system has almost perfect accounting mechanisms but is completely flawed because it only allows a privileged few to issue currency. This redistributes exponential debt to all participants in the currency without their direct consent or knowledge for reasons not under their control. Furthermore, its lack of real world commodities as backing make it impractical for measuring the value of goods due to its depreciating value. It is a fiat currency and its value is only "hype".

Humanity exists in a post Bitcoin world. Currency can be created and issued by anyone who can conceive of a token system. Therefore, participation in unfair systems is only currently being supported by ignorance of alternatives, ease of use, widespread adoption, and technical barriers. Bitcoin and many similar technologies contain technical hurdles that make it hard to participate in and have limitations that make them unscalable for all commercial purposes.

Prometheus aims at bridging the gap of the aforementioned barriers with the use of concepts drawn from social networking. Time is the unit by which all things are measured against either implicitly or explicitly making it the perfect commodity for measuring goods and valuing currency. A central bank issues notes of currency as a promise of the bank to tender its value to the holder of the note. In very much the same way, Sparks, the currency of Prometheus, is a promise of a member to provide value relative to time to the holder of the sparks. Sparks represent -1 second of a member's time to be shared with other member. Commitments of time to other people is an interaction humans make with ease every day for both commercial and interpersonal interactions. How humanity spends its time and what it spends it on is the only capital that truly matters.

As a social network Prometheus will provide the ability for members to connect and interact on a commercial level and broker transactions. By using smart contracts as a means of solidifying transactions members can deterministically decide how their money moves at their discretion. Members will buy, sell, trade, and fund products in any way they can perceive and represent in code. Tools like Craigslist, Ebay, Kickstarter, and Patreon currently exhibit similar functionality but Prometheus' functionality is determined by its user base making its potential completely open.

Prometheus is developed in completely open source to ensure its accountability and to be resilient to change and the commercial needs of its members.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Prometheus?
Prometheus is a social network that allows you to pay other members of the network for the products of their labor. Payment can only be made in the most valuable commodity in existence, time. Prometheus is a platform for a new type of economy, a volunteerist economy. This economy is backed by the promise to provide value from its members in the form of goods and services measure in the time. (Gesundheit!)
How can time be currency?
People credit out their time as a medium of exchange. The currency can be thought of as being a voucher for the time of the person who credited it to whomever holds the voucher.
What are Sparks?
The spark(*) is the currency of the Prometheus network. A spark is worth -1 second of human life.
Are sparks exchangeable with other currencies?
Because time is a fixed commodity and fiat curencies are not backed by commodities exchange can not be made directly but must be facilitated through a valuable commodity.
Do you need to know how to program to use smart contracts?
We are developing a system to allow programming novices and afficianados to be able to accomplish smart contracts creation with ease.
